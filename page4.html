3.Ragdoll

Are a popular breed of domestic cat known for their long, flowing coat and docile, relaxed personality. They were developed in the 1960s in California and have since become one of the most popular breeds in the United States and many other countries. Ragdoll cats are our affectionate companions, with their velvety fur, big blue eyes, and friendly demeanor, they make great pets for families and singletons alike. They are generally healthy, but like all cats, they are prone to certain genetic disorders. They are generally easy to groom, and their coat doesn't shed much.

4.Russian Blue

Is a breed of domestic cat known for its striking appearance. They have blue-gray fur, green eyes, and a white patch on their chest. This breed is intelligent, active, and playful, making them great companions for families with children or other pets. They enjoy interactive toys and games and can be trained to perform tricks. Russian Blues are also relatively low-maintenance in terms of grooming and are generally healthy cats.
