Cats

Are one of the most popular pets in the world. They belong to the Felidae family and are characterized by their soft fur, cute faces, and playful personalities. Cats come in a wide variety of breeds, each with their own unique physical characteristics and temperaments. They are known for their agility, independence, and their ability to make great companions.
