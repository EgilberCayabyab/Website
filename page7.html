9.Bengal

Is a popular breed of domestic cat that was developed in the mid-20th century in the United States. It was bred to resemble the wild leopard cat of the Bengal Peninsula, resulting in a cat with a golden or silver coat with black spots and stripes. The breed is known for its athleticism, agility, and playfulness, as well as its loyalty and friendly nature. Bengal cats are medium to large in size, weighing between 8 to 18 pounds and reaching lengths of up to 36 inches. They are a relatively healthy breed with a lifespan of 12-16 years.

10.Himalayan

Is a breed of domestic cat that originated in the Himalayan mountains of Nepal. They are known for their thick, soft fur, which can come in a variety of colors and patterns, including solid colors, tabbies, and tortoiseshells. Himalayan cats have a friendly and affectionate temperament, and they get along well with children and other pets. They are also very intelligent and enjoy playing and interacting with their owners.
