1.Scottish Fold

The Scottish Fold is a breed of domestic cat, known for its unique angular, compact, and oval-shaped body, with the ears folded forward flat on the head, giving it an "owl-like" appearance. They are generally a small to medium-sized breed, with a short, thick coat that can come in a wide variety of colors and patterns. The breed is named after the Scottish border region where it was developed.

2.Maine Coon

The Maine Coon is a breed of domestic cat known for its large size, tufted ears, and bushy tail. They are one of the oldest natural breeds in North America and are characterized by their friendly and sociable nature. Maine Coons are often described as "gentle giants" and are known for their intelligence and playful temperament. They are also well-regarded for their hunting skills and adaptability to various environments. Maine Coons are a popular choice for cat lovers due to their affectionate nature and striking appearance.
