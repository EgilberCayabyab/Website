5.Siamese

Is a breed of domestic cat known for its sleek, distinctive body shape and color points on the ears, face, paws, and tail. They have a slender, muscular build and come in a variety of coat colors, including seal point, blue point, chocolate point, and lilac point. Siamese cats are known for their social and vocal nature, often forming strong bonds with their owners and communicating through meows and chirps. They also have a reputation for being intelligent and curious, often enjoying toys and tricks that challenge their minds and bodies.

6.British Shorthair

Are a popular breed of domestic cats from the United Kingdom. They have a dense, water-repellent coat that is typically short in length, ranging from three to six inches. Their fur is thick on the body and head, but lighter on their ears, paws, and underbelly. These cats have a compact, sturdy body, short legs, and a strong bone structure. British Shorthairs are known for their docile and friendly demeanor, making them great companions. They are intelligent, enjoys puzzles, and can adapt to changes in their environment. These cats require minimal grooming and have a low maintenance diet.
